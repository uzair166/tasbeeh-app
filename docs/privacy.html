Tasbeeh

Volume Button Counter

Effective Date: March 11, 2026

Summary

Tasbeeh does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Your dhikr counts and settings are stored locally on your device and, if you choose, synced through your own iCloud account. We have no servers, no analytics, and no way to access your data.

Data We Collect

None. Tasbeeh collects zero personal data. We do not use analytics services, advertising frameworks, or any third-party SDKs that collect data.

Data Stored on Your Device

The following data is stored locally on your iPhone using UserDefaults:

  • Dhikr count history (daily counts by date)
  • Lifetime count, streak, and personal records
  • Your dhikr presets and preferences
  • App settings (haptic intensity, sound, appearance)

This data never leaves your device unless you enable iCloud sync.

iCloud Sync

If you enable iCloud sync (on by default), your counts, streak, and history are synced across your Apple devices using Apple's NSUbiquitousKeyValueStore. This data is transmitted and stored entirely within Apple's iCloud infrastructure under your personal Apple ID.

We do not operate any servers. We cannot access, read, or modify your iCloud data. You can disable iCloud sync at any time in the app's settings.

Third-Party Services

Tasbeeh uses no third-party services. There are no analytics, no crash reporting, no advertising, and no external network calls. The app functions entirely offline.

Children's Privacy

Tasbeeh does not collect any data from anyone, including children. The app is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the changes will be posted on this page with a revised effective date. Since we collect no data, meaningful changes are unlikely.
